Analysis of Education, Healthcare and Military Expense of G-20 Countries

Project Description

This is an individual project analyzing the expense of Education, Healthcare and Military for 10 G-20 countries.

Overview

In this project, I first selected all the 20 countries from G-20 including European Union. The data is obtained from the World Bank Open Data. Unforturnately, the education expense data of many countries is missing, some of the those are very top countries, such as USA, Russia and China. Hence, I focus on those countries who has more than 6 years of education expense data between 2010 to 2016.


The data are obtained from the World Bank Open Data, and I queried the data by countries' name. After clean and process the data using a Python script, I uploaded the cleaned data to google sheet, and then use Google Chart API to generate the charts.
